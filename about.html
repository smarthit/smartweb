MISSION

We are a group of seasoned professionals excelling in various disciplines including technology, project management, healthcare, organizational change and development, marketing, training, _____ who have a common vision to positively impact the financial affordability of healthcare to patients and practices. Our aim is to bridge affordable healthcare to the market and enhance patient self-knowledge and engagement that begins the empowered conversation with practitioners that produce better healthcare options and outcome

Smart E HIT is an Information Services and Technology Consulting firm, providing Health Information Technology to medical practices and clinics.  Based in New York, we service clients from New England to Georgia and the Gulf Coast.

We provide clients with effective Health Information Management   technology based on  low-cost “Open EMR” systems and the know-how of our seasoned staff of Technologists, Health Information Managers, Practice Improvement  and Change Management experts.

We have a passion for Patient Engagement and open access along with a deep understanding of the technology and information issues, as well as the regulatory requirements for patient privacy (HIPAA), Meaningful Use (MU), etc.

We don’t just follow the latest industry hype, we rise above the confusion to craft affordable, effective Health Information Management and Practice Management solutions that help our client organizations – and their patients  – benefit from reliable and timely information.
