ICSA Certified

OpenEMR is a Free and Open Source electronic health records and medical practice management application that can run on Windows, Linux, Mac OS X, and many other platforms. OpenEMR is ONC certified and is one of the most popular open source electronic medical records in use today.

The OpenEMR community is dedicated to guarding OpenEMR's status as a free, open source software solution for medical practices.
